## Question Presented

What legal and contractual consequences follow from using a YouTube video,
description, channel, or linked download as research or sample-acquisition
provenance, and what permissions apply to downloaded files?

## Verified Baseline

A public video URL, download instruction, or uploader identity does not by
itself prove ownership of every linked file or grant redistribution rights.
Platform terms, uploader permission, copyright, and rights in embedded
third-party content must be analyzed separately.

The live United States-facing YouTube Terms of Service identify 2023-12-15 as
their effective date. That date was reverified at the current terms endpoint on
2026-07-13. The terms permit use of content through service features and
otherwise require authorization from the service, permission from the relevant
rights holders, or another applicable legal basis before downloading,
reproducing, distributing, modifying, or using content outside the ordinary
service experience. They also require uploaders to have permission or another
legal entitlement for included third-party intellectual property.

A video description may identify an external download, but that statement is
evidence only of what the uploader represented. The external host's terms, the
file's notices, the uploader's authority, and rights in every included component
remain separate questions.

## Not Established

This record does not establish that a public download is authorized by every
rights holder, that YouTube terms permit every download method, that an uploader
owns all linked material, or that local research permits republication.

## Required Facts

- Exact video ID, channel URL, title, description, and retrieval date.
- Exact instruction followed and whether YouTube or an external host supplied
  the file.
- File name, hash, size, download date, and any accompanying terms or notices.
- Uploader statements about ownership, permission, modification, and
  distribution.
- Third-party game, audio, translation, mod, and platform content in the file.

## Required Authorities

- [YouTube Terms of Service][youtube-terms], effective 15 December 2023, and
  applicable product policies (Accessed: 13 July 2026).
- Video-specific description, license indicator, and uploader statements.
- Terms and license notices at the actual download host.
- Written permission from the relevant rights holder when relied upon.

## Verification Checklist

- Preserve public metadata and the exact acquisition instruction privately.
- Do not copy video, transcript, thumbnail, or description into SHAR by default.
- Separate permission to download from permission to modify or redistribute.
- Verify ownership and authority for every material component.
- Obtain qualified counsel before publishing a sample or derivative content.
