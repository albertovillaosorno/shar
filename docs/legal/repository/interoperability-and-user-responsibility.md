# Interoperability And User Responsibility Boundary

> [Legal Research Disclaimer](../disclaimer.md) applies to this record.

- Status: Repository risk boundary; legal outcomes remain fact-specific.
- Jurisdiction: User- and conduct-specific.
- As-of date: 2026-07-12.
- Counsel review: Not performed.

## Purpose

SHAR is an independent interoperability and reimplementation project. This
record limits repository conduct; it does not declare that any particular
reverse-engineering, modification, conversion, or distribution is lawful.

## Repository Boundary

- Users supply and retain lawful access to their own inputs.
- Original game source, executables, assets, audio, artwork, internal documents,
  credentials, and extracted payloads remain outside Git.
- Published code must be independently authored or otherwise licensed for
  publication.
- Private evidence may inform functional understanding but must not be copied
  into public implementation or documentation.
- Access-control circumvention, encrypted-content extraction, private service
  endpoints, credential use, cheating, malware, and unauthorized access are
  outside the approved repository workflow.
- Generated artifacts remain subject to rights in their source content and are
  not presumed redistributable.
- No third-party affiliation, endorsement, sponsorship, or approval is claimed.

## Legal Review Boundary

Potentially relevant United States authorities include 17 U.S.C. §§ 102(b), 107,
117, and 1201, together with the cases indexed in `docs/legal/cases/`. None is
treated as blanket permission. Other jurisdictions and agreed terms may produce
different results.

## Stop Conditions

Stop publication or disputed distribution when lawful access, input rights,
copied expression, access controls, assent, redistribution authority, or the
controlling jurisdiction is materially unclear.
