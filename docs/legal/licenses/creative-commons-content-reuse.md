## Question Presented

When may SHAR quote, adapt, translate, or otherwise reuse text or media offered
under a Creative Commons license, and what attribution, share-alike,
noncommercial, no-derivatives, notice, and indication-of-changes obligations
apply?

## Verified Baseline

A site-wide statement may not control every page, revision, imported passage, or
media file. Text and non-text media can carry different licenses. Attribution
and license compatibility must be evaluated for the exact material reused.

Fandom's current licensing page states that community text is generally licensed
under CC BY-SA 3.0 Unported unless a particular wiki uses an approved different
license. The same page states that discussion and chat content is not licensed
under the community CC terms and that non-text media must be checked on its own
description page. Fandom's terms distinguish community text from
company-original content and other user-content grants.

CC BY-SA 3.0 permits licensed reuse and adaptation subject to the legal code,
including attribution, license notice, indication of changes, and ShareAlike
conditions for adaptations. The license does not cover material that the
purported licensor had no authority to license, nor does a page-level text
license automatically cover embedded third-party media.

## Not Established

This record does not establish that every Fandom page is CC BY-SA, that all
media shares the text license, that attribution to a wiki name alone is
sufficient, or that research access authorizes copying into SHAR.

## Required Facts

- Exact page, permanent revision, authors, edit history, and retrieval date.
- License displayed for that page and for every reused media file.
- Imported text, third-party notices, and additional attribution requirements.
- Whether SHAR copies, adapts, translates, summarizes, or merely cites.
- Distribution format, commercial context, and compatibility with SHAR
  licensing.

## Required Authorities

- Exact Creative Commons legal code for the stated license and version.
- Page-specific license notice and permanent revision history.
- Media-description page for each image, audio, or video file.
- Fandom licensing terms and applicable terms of use.
