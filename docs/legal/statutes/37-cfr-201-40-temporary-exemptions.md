## Question Presented

Does a current exemption apply to one exact SHAR act, user, work class, purpose,
and date, and what limitations remain outside that exemption?

## Current Rulemaking Posture

The ninth triennial proceeding concluded with the October 28, 2024 final rule.
The resulting classes are codified in 37 C.F.R. § 201.40 and remain the
operative temporary exemptions during their current term.

The Copyright Office initiated the tenth triennial proceeding for the 2027 cycle
on June 9, 2026. Renewal and new-exemption petitions are due August 24, 2026,
and comments responding to renewal petitions are due September 28, 2026. Any
renewed exemptions would govern October 2027 through October 2030. Opening the
new proceeding does not suspend or enlarge the current 2024 exemptions.

## Legal Boundary

The triennial exemptions implement the limitation in § 1201(a)(1)(B) through
(D). They concern the act of circumventing an access control for users and
classes meeting exact regulatory conditions. Section 1201(a)(1)(E) states that a
rulemaking determination may not be used as a defense to provisions of Title 17
other than § 1201(a)(1).

A temporary exemption therefore does not by itself:

- authorize trafficking under § 1201(a)(2) or § 1201(b);
- establish fair use, authorization, ownership, or noninfringement;
- override a contract, access law, trade-secret duty, or other applicable law;
- convert an individual preservation activity into a general distribution right;
  or
- apply outside its defined users, works, purposes, conditions, and dates.

## SHAR Evidence Required

For each proposed reliance, record:

1. The exact paragraph of 37 C.F.R. § 201.40.
1. The work class and lawfully acquired or licensed copy.
1. The person or institution qualifying as the permitted user.
1. The precise technological measure and circumvention act.
1. The sole or limited purpose stated by the class.
1. Every preservation, security, access, location, distribution, or commercial
   condition.
1. The conduct date and regulation then in force.
1. Tool development or sharing analyzed separately under the trafficking rules.
1. The underlying noninfringing-use and other-law analysis.

## Repository Relevance

The current video-game preservation classes are institution- and condition-
specific. They do not establish that an individual repository operator may
circumvent a game access control for general development, modding, publication,
or online distribution. The exact class text must be quoted and matched before
any reliance.
