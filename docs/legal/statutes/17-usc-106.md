## Question Presented

Which exclusive rights are implicated by SHAR's reproduction, intermediate
copying, adaptation, extraction, transformation, display, performance, and
distribution activities for each distinct input and output?

## Verified Baseline

Section 106 defines exclusive rights subject to limitations elsewhere in
Title 17. A technical act can implicate more than one right, and identifying a
right does not decide whether a license, limitation, defense, or exception
applies.

## Not Established

This record does not establish infringement, ownership, substantial similarity,
a valid registration, distribution, public display, public performance, or the
absence of an applicable limitation or defense.
