# SHAR Legal Authorities And Repository Boundaries

See the [SHAR Documentation Guide](../README.md) for documentation ownership,
maintenance, validation, and public-content rules.

> [Legal Research Disclaimer](disclaimer.md) is the single source of truth for
> the scope and permitted use of these records.

This directory contains dated authority summaries, fact-specific application
boundaries, and repository publication constraints. Use the
[legal record template](template.md) as the starting structure for new records;
case, statute, and specialized records may add narrower metadata and sections.

Technical architecture decisions remain in [`docs/adr`](../adr/). Legal analysis
may cite ADRs for technical facts, but ADRs are not legal authority.

## Source And Review Rule

Every record distinguishes verified authority from facts that have not been
established for a specific product copy, agreement, act, output, territory, or
distribution event. A complete authority record may therefore state that a
fact-specific result is not determined. Hosting, public access, noncommercial
purpose, independent implementation, interoperability intent, or an accepted ADR
does not determine a legal outcome by itself.
