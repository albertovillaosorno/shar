## Covered Material

GitHub hosting and ordinary collaboration services used for the public SHAR
repository.

## Repository Use And Scope

GitHub hosts the public repository. Hosting does not replace an explicit
repository license and does not grant rights in third-party content. The SHAR
MIT License applies only to material the repository owner has authority to
license.

## Provenance And Version History

GitHub's current Terms of Service and Additional Product Terms identify April
27, 2026 as their effective date. Both were re-reviewed on 14 July 2026. The
exact account type, enabled features, AI-input opt-out state, organization
relationship, enterprise or volume agreement, and historical or feature-specific
agreement remain facts of the relevant account and use event. Current terms must
not be applied retroactively without evidence.

## Authorship, Ownership, And Attribution

GitHub's current terms state that users retain ownership of content they post
and remain responsible for having the rights needed to submit it. GitHub retains
rights in its service, site, software, documentation, and branding. Public
visibility does not establish ownership or permission beyond the governing
repository license, third-party rights, and service functionality.

## License Or Terms Basis

Use is governed by GitHub's Terms of Service and any applicable additional or
feature-specific terms. The current terms grant GitHub and its affiliates a
service license that expressly includes developing, training, and improving AI
and machine-learning models. They separately grant other users service-limited
rights to use, display, perform, and reproduce public-repository content through
GitHub's fork functionality.

For individual licenses, the AI-feature input and output license has an account
opt-out, but the terms state that this opt-out does not limit the broader
repository-content licenses. The terms also contain a separate
access-reciprocity
clause for automated access to public content used to develop or train a
commercially available AI system, subject to stated academic-research and
monthly-active-user exceptions. None of these platform provisions replaces the
repository's outbound license or supplies rights in third-party material.
Detailed application limits are recorded in
[GitHub Hosting And Terms](../../legal/platforms/github-hosting-and-terms.md).

## Distribution, Modification, And Compatibility

Only lawfully publishable repository material may be hosted. Original game
files, extracted assets, non-public research evidence, credentials, and
proprietary tool payloads remain excluded. Additional AI, marketplace, package,
release, or API features require feature-specific review.

## Compliance Posture

Before publication, verify the repository license, contributor authority,
secret absence, asset exclusions, and every third-party notice. Treat the
individual AI-input opt-out as narrower than the public-repository content
license, and review the access-reciprocity clause before automated
public-content
collection for commercial AI training.
