# Bibliography Research Disclaimer

The material under `docs/bibliography/` is a non-governing evidence and
provenance catalog. It records source identity, observed versions, repository
relationships, license or terms evidence, proposition-level confidence, and
known limits. It does not make architecture decisions, determine legal outcomes,
or authorize operational conduct.

A lifecycle status such as `Evidence recorded` means that a bounded evidence
account is complete enough to retain. It does not mean every proposition is
verified. The separate evidence-status field controls confidence, and explicit
uncertainty remains part of the record.

Public availability, a citation, attribution, successful parsing, local
possession, or a download instruction does not establish authorship, ownership,
a license, permission, enforceability, or redistribution rights. A bibliography
record does not authorize copying, modification, circumvention, publication,
streaming, monetization, or distribution.

Private samples, extracted contents, credentials, machine-specific routes,
private hashes, transient version announcements, and direct delivery locations
remain outside public documentation unless independently authorized and
necessary for a lawful public purpose.

The [Legal Research Disclaimer](../legal/disclaimer.md) is the single canonical
notice for legal scope, no-advice boundaries, and fact-specific legal reliance.
This file supplements that notice only by defining bibliography-specific
non-authority; it is not a second or alternative legal disclaimer.
